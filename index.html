Ballistic Angels is a recycling music project. Here are some tracks under development.

The Eliot Tracks

Tracks with vocal samples from T.S. Eliot treated with a classic cut-up technique and extensive sample editing. Eliot recorded his master­pieces The Waste Land and The Hollow Men starting in 1923, a couple of years before Blind Lemon Jefferson recorded his first tracks and over a decade before Robert Johnson did so.
